Who Was Buzz Aldrin?

Buzz Aldrin is an American astronaut and engineer who made history as one of the first two humans to land on the Moon during NASA's Apollo 11 mission. On July 20, 1969, Aldrin and mission commander Neil Armstrong descended to the lunar surface in the Lunar Module, named "Eagle." Armstrong became the first person to set foot on the Moon, followed by Aldrin shortly after.
