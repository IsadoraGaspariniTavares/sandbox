Healthy Eating

Healthy eating means providing all the fundamental nutrients for the functioning of our body, such as carbohydrates, lipids, protein, mineral salts, and vitamins

The key to having a healthy diet is balance. We cannot exclude any of the nutrients from our diet, but we must be aware of the amount of everthing that we ingest. There is no way of compensating the lack of one nutrient by ingesting another one in larger quantities.

Benefits of Healthy Eating

Healthy eating is essential for the functioning of our body, because it is from a balanced diet that we get the nutrients we need for our survival. A healthy diet is related to several benefits for our body, such as the improvement in immunity, mood and sleep quality, the increase in disposition, the regulation of our intestine, and the maintenance of weight.

In addition, we must not forget that an unbalanced diet can help develop problems such as obesity, hyertension, diabetes, cardiovascular diseases, and even certain types of cancer. Investing in proper nutrition is, therefore, investing in your health.

Surveys found that unhealthy diets were responsible for almost 11 million deaths in 2017, compared to 10.4 million deaths caused by high blood preassure and 8 million by cigarettes.
